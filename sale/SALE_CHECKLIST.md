## Step 2: Build Credibility (Week 2-3, parallel)

- [ ] **Get 50-100 users** — Share on Facebook groups, Reddit r/Romania, friends
- [ ] **Get 3-5 paying customers** — Even 5 RON/month proves willingness to pay
- [ ] **LinkedIn posts** — Post 2-3 times about the product, AI health, Romanian market
- [ ] **ProductHunt / HackerNews** — Launch for visibility and backlinks

## Step 3: Outreach (Week 3-6)

- [ ] **Warm intros** — Check personal network for contacts at target companies
- [ ] **LinkedIn direct messages** — CTOs/Product leads at Tier 1 companies (SanoPass, MedLife, NN Insurance, Regina Maria)
- [ ] **Email pitches** — Formal inquiry to Tier 1 and Tier 2 companies
- [ ] **Health-tech events** — Innovation in Health, eHealth Forum Romania
- [ ] **Apply to SeedBlink / TechAngels** — For investor conversations → buyer connections

## Step 4: Negotiate (Week 6-10)

- [ ] **Decide: asset sale vs company sale** — If no SRL, asset sale only
- [ ] **Prepare licensing option** — Some companies prefer monthly license over acquisition
- [ ] **Legal review** — NDA template, LOI template, asset purchase agreement
- [ ] **Define transition terms** — 3-6 months support, knowledge transfer, handoff
